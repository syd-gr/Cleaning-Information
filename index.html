Task Before Starting Work

Prepare all necessary cleaning and bed making tools.

Check your Job Responsibilities
As per Your Position

Responsibilities are categorized into Three stages: Linen_and_Waste_Management, Bed-Making and Bathroom_Cleaning Please review all Responsibilities to fully understand the rules before starting your work.

  • Linen_and_Waste_Management
  • Bed-Making
  • Bathroom_Cleaning

  • Linen_and_Waste
    Management job Responsibilities

    1. Collect, sort, and distribute clean linens and towels to designated areas.

    2. Remove soiled or used linens, towels, and bedding for proper laundering.

    3. Ensure linen storage areas are stocked, organized, and well-maintained.

    4. Dispose of waste, including trash and used personal hygiene items, following sanitation and safety protocols.

    5. Monitor linen inventory and report shortages or damages to supervisors.

    6. Handle biohazard waste appropriately, adhering to health and safety regulations.

    7. Assist in maintaining laundry carts and ensure proper handling of fresh linens.

  • Bed-Making job Responsibilities

    1. Make beds with fresh linens, ensuring they are neat, wrinkle-free, and properly tucked in.

    2. Change bed sheets, pillowcases, and blankets according to hotel/hospital guidelines.

    3. Arrange pillows, bedspreads, and decorative elements as per standards.

    4. Inspect bedding for stains, damages, or wear and replace them if necessary.

    5. Ensure mattress protectors are in place and maintained properly, Maintain cleanliness and hygiene around the bed area.

    6. Follow specific bed-making standards for hospitals, hotels, or hospitality settings.

  • Bathroom_Cleaning job Responsibilities

    1. Clean and disinfect sinks, toilets, showers, bathtubs, and bathroom floors.

    2. Refill bathroom supplies such as toilet paper, soap, shampoo, and towels.

    3. Remove and replace used towels and bath mats with fresh ones.

    4. Wipe down mirrors, countertops, and other surfaces to ensure a polished appearance.

    5. Report any maintenance issues like leaks, clogged drains, or broken fixtures.

    6. Empty trash bins and properly dispose of waste, Ensure a fresh and odor-free environment by using appropriate cleaning agents.
